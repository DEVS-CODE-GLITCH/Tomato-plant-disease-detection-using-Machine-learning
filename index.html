About Tomato Plant Disease Detection

The Tomato Plant Disease Detection System is an innovative solution that utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify diseases affecting tomato plants. Trained on a dataset comprising over 13,000 images belonging to six distinct disease classes, this system empowers farmers and agricultural experts to swiftly identify and manage diseases, thus enhancing crop yield and quality.

With its robust training on diverse disease patterns, the system can accurately diagnose common tomato plant diseases, including:

  • Bacterial Spot
  • Early Blight
  • Healthy (no disease)
  • Late Blight
  • Septoria Leaf Spot
  • Yellow Leaf Curl Virus
